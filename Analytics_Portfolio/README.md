# CASE STUDY: Yulu Demand Optimization

**Title:** How Yulu Can Optimize Demand & Utilization Using Data

**Problem:** Fluctuating demand leading to underutilized fleet and inconsistent user experience

**Metrics:** 
* Ride Count,
* Utilization Rate,
* Demand by Time/Season,
* Weather Impact

**Analysis:** 
* Exploratory Data Analysis + Hypothesis Testing to evaluate impact of seasonality,
* weather, and working days on demand

**Insights:**
* Demand significantly varies across seasons and weather conditions
* Higher usage observed on working days and favorable weather
* Identified predictable peak demand patterns 

**Business Recommendations:**
* Implement demand-based fleet allocation across locations and time slots
* Optimize operations for peak vs low-demand periods
* Improve availability during high-demand scenarios to boost utilization and revenue
